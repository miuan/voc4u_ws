How it's works

First step is download into your smartphone :-) After download and first start you must choise your native (language which you understand) and second you must select language which you want learn. Take patience, because in first start the application prepare database with your choisen words.
The choising native and learn language you do only once. When you want change the language setting, you must go to the dictionary a set "Reset DB".
The voc4u have three options.
#1: Train is the main and there you can improve memory of vocabulary, for examle in public transport.
#2: Speaker mod is about, vocabulary to speech and you can only listening, for examle when you go to bet.
#3: For otherwise here is mode for trying pronunciation, the Phone listening you and comparing with the regular word.
Main think of Voc4u is train mode. The train mode have only two buttons. The buttos are "know" and "don't know". Any buttons show next word (sometime time in learn languege, sometime in native langugae ). You must guess true the pair for this word. When you are sure with the pair, take "know" if you are not sure press "don't know". The main andvatage of Voc4u is if you mark as "don't know" the market word will be show more fruequently to you, and otherwise words marked as "know" once total disapear.
You must know, when you touch to the list with the words (pairs). The Voc4u will speech the you learn part to you for improve your pronoucation.
From train is possible go from menu to dictionary and there you can choice the group of learn words. You can choice from for parts (Essetnial, Elementary, Pre-Intermediate, Intermediate) and also you can add your words if you want ;-) . The words in the groups you can see vocabulary where you can help you with the improve database.
The showed words in groups aren't all, becouse any group have more as 400 words!!!
