About

Voc4u is application for smartphones with operating system Android and Bada (we also plan go to the another platforms). It is simple (see to how it's works) vocabulary for learning words in cross languages. Now you can learn Czech, English, France, German, Italian, Spain, Poland and Portugal. Visit the markets and download the application to your smartphone for free ;-)
Main andvantage for Voc4u is: only two buttons for learning, "know" and "don't know" buttons which you mark as "don't know" will be show more fruequently to you, and otherwise words marked as "know" once total disapear.
